# Client Onboarding Workflow

This SOP handles the end-to-end process of onboarding a new client.

1. **Create Company in CRM**: Create a new company record in HubSpot.
2. **Create Contact in CRM**: Create a contact person for the company in HubSpot.
3. **Create Contact in Xero**: Create a corresponding contact record in Xero for billing.
4. **Create Invoice**: Generate an initial onboarding invoice in Xero.
5. **Create Onboarding Project**: Create a new project/issue in Linear to track onboarding tasks.
